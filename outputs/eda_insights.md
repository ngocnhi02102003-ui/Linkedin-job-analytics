# EDA Insights: LinkedIn Jobs Analysis

## A. Dataset Overview

- **Total Job Postings**: 123,849
- **Unique Companies**: 24,475
- **Unique Industries**: 409
- **Avg Skills per Job**: 1.66
- **Salary Disclosed %**: 29.12%
- **Salary Hidden %**: 70.88%
- **Remote %**: 12.31%
- **Sponsored %**: 0.00%
- **Date Range**: 2024-03-24 to 2024-04-20

## B. Key Descriptive Findings

1. **Salary Transparency Gap**: Only 29.1% of jobs disclose salary.
2. **Remote Premium**: Remote jobs offer higher median salary than on-site roles.
3. **Experience Ladder**: Executive roles pay ~3x more than Entry-level positions.

## C. Diagnostic Findings

1. **Why hide salary?** Industries with high talent competition tend to hide salary more.
2. **Skills Effect**: Weak positive correlation between skill count and salary (r ≈ 0.1).
3. **Engagement Pattern**: Jobs with disclosed salary in Mid-High bands get highest engagement.
